About Githmi Peiris Designs

Githmi Peiris

BA (Hons) in Interior Architecture

Limkokwing University of Creative Technology, Malaysia
 

Githmi Peiris is a visionary Interior Architect holding a BA (Hons) from Malaysia's Limkokwing University. With over seven years of elite industry experience, she infuses premium residential, luxury commercial, and boutique hospitality sectors with meticulous structural detail and an empathetic approach.

Established in 2015, Sri Lanka-based Githmi Peiris Designs is a premier, full-service interior architecture firm celebrated for a 100% satisfaction record across 450+ global projects. The elite team acts as design storytellers, seamlessly delivering bespoke structural solutions and high-end products impeccably on schedule.
